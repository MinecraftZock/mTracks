Motocross track directory for Europe (~2100) and USA (~1700),
actual tracks from following countries are in the app: Germany, Austria, Netherlands, Belgium, France, Switzerland, Italy, Spain, Sweden, Denmark, Norway, Czech, Poland, Hungarian and Great Britain.

Tracks can be searched by name, event or country

Not finding any track is past, now you drive directly to it, without spending extra time for searching it

Tracks are displayed as list (order by name or distance) or displayed on a map, with additive distance to actual position.

Useful information are included, soil type, length, fees an opening hours (if provided from track owner) as well.

With a touch event, track information can be required with mail, web or phone from the track owner as well
Coordinates can be shared with friends or other guys
